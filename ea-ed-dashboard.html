How To Read This

It Is A Decision Lens, Not A Ranking

The sheet is comparing where an early application might be strategically sensible. A strong school can still be the wrong early choice if soccer pressure, academic load, travel, testing risk, and financial strategy do not line up with Siyi's current profile.

Use This For Questions, Not Answers

Before choosing ED or EA, convert each school's row into three facts: coach signal, academic pre-read viability, and test-submission strategy. A school with a beautiful fit on paper should not receive ED unless at least two of those three facts are favorable.
